Work and past experiences

Between coding, web design, management and photography, these are the main subjects im trying to master to propel my career into the future.

So far I had some experiences such as being a self-employed driver, shop assistant, commercial model and condominium administrator. Each and every single one helped me develop different skills that I still use every single day in order to complete tasks at hand, and look at problems through different perspectives.

As a self-employed driver, I had to create my own company with a partner that specific business. This was one of the biggest challenges I had in my life so far. From understanding all the financial responsibilities, to developing a relationship with an accountant, trying to promote my services and even dealing with unexpected events such as not being able to work due to breakdown and some other unfortunate events. But hey, that's part of being a manager in itself.

If I had to pick the most stressful and less enjoyable experience it had to be the shop assistant experience. From a customer perspective I had no idea the amount of work that is involved in working in a clothing store. From sorting a warehouse where you have to be organized, methodical, and very focused on the task at hand, to being exposed to the public where you have to manage expectations. I learned a lot in a short amount of time.
